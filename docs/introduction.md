# 1. Introduction

In today's world, inflation leads to the devaluation of savings, and governments are introducing new fees for banking transactions (for example, in Slovakia). The unstable global situation also increases the demand for decentralized systems that provide users with financial independence and privacy.

## 1.1 Relevance of Decentralized Systems

### Financial Freedom

Central banks and governments can control traditional financial systems and introduce new taxes and fees.  
Decentralized solutions allow users full control over their assets without intermediaries.

### Reduced Fees

Bank transfers often include high fees, especially for international transactions.  
Decentralized networks offer faster and cheaper transactions.

### Protection Against Inflation

In times of rising inflation, traditional currencies lose their value.  
Decentralized assets can serve as an alternative store of value.

### Reliability and Resistance to Failures

Centralized systems are vulnerable to censorship, account freezing, and technical outages.  
In decentralized networks, data is distributed across many nodes, ensuring stability and security.

---

## 1.2 Ethical Aspects and Prevention of Illegal Use

When discussing decentralized solutions, questions often arise regarding their potential misuse for illegal activities (e.g., black markets on the dark web). The following aspects should be considered:

### Decentralization ≠ Anonymity

In most blockchains, transactions are publicly traceable, making them less anonymous than cash payments.  
Analytical tools can be used to monitor transactions and detect suspicious activity.

### Legitimate Use of the Technology

Decentralized systems help millions of people in countries with weak economies and restrictive regimes gain access to financial services.  
They enable international payments and protect users from censorship and political pressure.

### Responsibility for Usage

Like the internet or cryptocurrencies, the technology itself is neutral — its use depends on how it is applied.  
To prevent illegal activities, regulatory mechanisms and compliance tools can be integrated into the system.

---

## 1.3 Conclusion

Decentralized systems represent a step into the future, where users can manage their finances without relying on centralized institutions. They offer new opportunities worldwide and help protect people from inflation, high fees, and financial restrictions. However, like any technology, they require responsible use and can be adapted for legal and secure applications.
